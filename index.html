About us

Founded in 1997 by a team of India’s leading cardiologists, Our Hospitals started its journey as a 100-bedded Heart Institute with a core team of 20 cardiologists, 1 operating theatre, and 1 catheterization laboratory. 24 years later, it is a multi-speciality healthcare provider with 14 healthcare facilities 6 cities across 5 states of India. It is the regional leader and is amongst the top 5 Pan-Indian hospital chains. Our Hospitals delivers comprehensive care in over 30 specialities. Adopting a service-oriented delivery model, provides cost-effective medical care, with an uncompromising commitment to its core purpose – ‘To make quality healthcare accessible at affordable costs to all’
